# Solar Reports

Solar conditions directly affect HF propagation. These reports correlate real
solar indices with measured spot data — not theoretical models. Every solar
value shown here is joined against actual signal measurements at 3-hour
resolution.

---

## Pages

- [**Current Conditions**](current.md) — Live SFI, Kp, SSN, and X-ray flux
  with 7-day trends and current band impact assessment.
- [**Storm Impact**](storms.md) — Measured SNR degradation during geomagnetic
  storms (Kp >= 5), by band, with recovery timelines.
- [**Solar Cycle 25**](cycle.md) — Where we are in the current cycle.
  Monthly SSN/SFI tracking and IONIS prediction accuracy over time.
