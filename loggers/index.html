    Contest Logger Statistics¶

    Analysis of 3508 logging software families extracted from 495,984 Cabrillo log submissions across 15 major HF contests, 2005–2025.

    Updated 12:00 UTC 2026-03-30

    Data Source

    Every Cabrillo log file contains a CREATED-BY: header identifying the logging software. We parse these headers from 496K+ logs downloaded from CQ and ARRL contest results. No operator identifiers are stored — this is aggregate statistics only.

    • N1MM Logger+ dominates with 45.37% of all submissions

    • Every top logger is Windows-only — macOS and Linux have < 1% combined share
    • The long tail includes 3498 additional logger families

    Platform Gap¶

    The contest logging market is 100% Windows at the top. macOS options (SkookumLogger, RUMlogNG, MacLoggerDX) and Linux options (TLF, CQRLOG, YFKtest) together represent less than 1% of submissions.

    This isn't a sample bias — it's the entire population of submitted Cabrillo logs from 15 major contests over 20 years.
